Medic 138

Medic 138 is a street-level EMS education project.

It exists for three groups:

  • EMT students who feel lost in PowerPoints
  • Paramedic students who want pattern recognition, not trivia
  • Working medics who still care enough to get better between calls

Lessons are short, scenario-based, and built for real shifts:

  • Warm-up scenario to set the scene
  • Core explanation in plain language
  • Interactive quiz, case, or game
  • Cheat sheet you can screenshot and reference later

Everything here is built by a working paramedic and instructor who still remembers what it feels like to be new.
